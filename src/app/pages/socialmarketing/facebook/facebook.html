What is Facebook Marketing?

Facebook marketing involves promoting your business, products, or services on Facebook to reach and engage with your target audience. With over 3 billion monthly active users, Facebook provides a vast platform for businesses to build brand awareness, drive website traffic, generate leads, and increase sales.

What is a Brand Ambassador?

A Brand Ambassador is a person who represents a brand and promotes its products or services to their followers. They can be celebrities, influencers, or thought leaders in their respective fields.

They serve as a favourable spokesperson to enhance brand recognition and boost sales. This role may include wearing or utilizing the company's products, sharing personal experiences with their network, and promoting the brand across social media or various other platforms.

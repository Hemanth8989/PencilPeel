What is Movie Promotion?

Movie Promotion, also known as Film Promotion or Movie Marketing, refers to the process of advertising and publicizing a film to attract audiences and generate interest in the movie. The goal of movie promotion is to create buzz, build anticipation, and ultimately drive ticket sales.
